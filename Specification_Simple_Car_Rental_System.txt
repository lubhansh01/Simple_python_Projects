Build a Car Rental System
Objective: Implement a simple car rental system with classes for cars and rentals.

Steps:

Define a Car Class:

Attributes: make, model, year, rental_rate, availability
Methods: rent_car(), return_car(), display_info()
Define a Rental Class:

Attributes: rental_id, car, customer_name, rental_date, return_date, total_cost
Methods: calculate_total_cost(), display_rental_info()
Implement Car Rental and Return:

In the Car class, implement the rent_car() and return_car() methods to manage the availability of the car.
In the Rental class, implement the calculate_total_cost() method to calculate the cost based on the rental rate and duration.
Manage Rental Records:

Maintain a list of rentals and update it whenever a car is rented or returned.
Test the System:

Create instances of Car and Rental.
Simulate renting and returning cars, and display the information of cars and rentals.
