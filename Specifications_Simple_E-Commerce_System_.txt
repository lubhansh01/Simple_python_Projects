Develop a Simple E-Commerce System
Objective: Implement a basic e-commerce system with classes for products and customers.

Steps:

Define a Product Class:

Attributes: name, price, stock
Methods: purchase(quantity), restock(quantity), display_info()
Define a Customer Class:

Attributes: name, customer_id, cart (dictionary with product and quantity)
Methods: add_to_cart(product, quantity), remove_from_cart(product), checkout()
Implement Cart Management:

In the Customer class, implement the add_to_cart() and remove_from_cart() methods to manage the products in the cart.
Implement Checkout:

In the Customer class, implement the checkout() method to calculate the total price, update the stock of products, and clear the cart.
Test the System:

Create instances of Product and Customer.
Simulate adding products to the cart, removing products, and checking out.
Display the information of products and customers.
